Create Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich!

The delightful duo of a juicy, grilled cheese sandwich is among the top-tier of comfort food combinations. This comforting food, while often high in fat, can be tweaked for a healthier version. Our grilled cheese variation is inspired by the delicious Southern classic, pimento cheese, cleverly incorporating nutritious elements like roasted peppers and Greek yogurt to offset the caloric content of the cheese. Enjoy this comforting recipe with just half the usual calories.

The Grilled Pimento Cheese Sandwich

Here are the ingredients and step by step process for making the item that will serve 4 people.

Ingredients

Name Quantity
Finely shredded sharp Cheddar cheese 1 1⁄4 cups
Diced pimentos 1 jar (4 oz)
Jalapeño pepper, seeded and minced 1
Finely sliced scallions 1⁄4 cup
2% Greek yogurt 1⁄4 cup
Olive-oil mayonnaise 1 Tbsp
Bread 8 slices
Butter Your choice

Directions

  1. Combine the cheese, pimentos, jalapeño, scallions, yogurt, mayonnaise, and a few shakes of Tabasco in a mixing bowl.
  2. Spread the mixture on 4 slices of bread and top with the remaining 4 slices.
  3. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Spread the outside of each sandwich with butter and cook until golden brown on both sides and the cheese is melted, about 3 minutes per side.
  4. Cut each sandwich in half and enjoy!
